# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone.

## Our standards

- Be respectful and considerate
- Assume good intent
- Provide constructive feedback
- Harassment or discriminatory behavior is not tolerated

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and discussions.
